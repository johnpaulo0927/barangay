National Women's Month Celebration!

March 18, 2024

To celebrate National Women’s Month, we are honored to conduct a series of special services to acknowledge the invaluable contributions and unwavering resilience of our brave women, Thank you so much to the LGBTQIA+ Under the leadership of Pres. Aris Rizal and the Sangguinang Barangay ng Sampiruhan, led by our esteemed Captain JP Dumalaon.

International Women’s Month is celebrated to honor the achievements and contributions of women throughout history and contemporary society. It serves as a platform to raise awareness about gender equality issues, advocate for women’s rights, and promote initiatives aimed at empowering women and girls worldwide. The celebration also highlights the ongoing struggles and challenges faced by women, including gender-based discrimination, violence, and unequal access to opportunities. By dedicating an entire month to celebrating women’s accomplishments and addressing gender inequality, International Women's Month aims to inspire positive change and foster a more inclusive and equitable society for all genders.

Kalinga at Inisyatiba para sa Malinis na Bayan Program

Jan 6, 2024

Pakikiisa ng Sangguniang Barangay ng Sampiruhan sa "Kalinga at Inisyatiba para sa Malinis na Bayan" Program. #CleanCommunities#KalinisanSaBagongPilipinas

Ang Kalinga Inisyatiba para sa malinis na Bayan ay isang proyektong naglalayong mapanatili at mapalaganap ang kalinisan sa mga komunidad sa buong bansa. Ito ay ginagawa upang mapangalagaan ang kalusugan ng mga mamamayan, mapigilan ang pagkalat ng sakit, at mapanatili ang kaayusan at kagandahan ng mga lugar. Sa pamamagitan ng pagtutulungan ng pamahalaan at mga mamamayan, ang inisyatibang ito ay naglalayong magkaroon ng maayos at malinis na kapaligiran para sa lahat.
